About NaijaFactCheck


Fake news spreads faster than truth. NaijaFactCheck was created to empower everyday Nigerians to easily verify WhatsApp messages, political claims, viral news, and scams — right from their browser.

Using AI scoring, keyword detection, and JSON-based APIs, we simulate a smart fact-checking assistant. This project was built for the 3MTT Knowledge Showcase.

🎯 Our Mission

👁️‍🗨️ Awareness

Expose the patterns behind deceptive WhatsApp broadcasts, political propaganda, and scam trends.

🛡️ Simplicity

Anyone with a phone and internet should be able to check a message in seconds — no login or stress required.

🤖 AI + Community

We combine AI logic and keyword filtering with local knowledge of Nigerian misinformation tactics.

🧠 How It Works

Messages are analyzed based on red-flag keywords (e.g., “Click here”, “FG palliative”), shortened links (like bit.ly), and suspicious patterns. Gemini AI (free version) and simulated API responses are used to mimic advanced features without heavy costs.

🙌 Built by a Missionary Learner

Created by Uduakobong Okonah, a Nigerian missionary learner passionate about software development and using tech for truth. This is more than a project — it's a mission to fight disinformation.
